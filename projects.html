Mini User Experience Project

Jan 2023 - May 2023

Conducted my own User Experience (UX) project by performing three contextual interviews on their note-taking practices. This involved immersing myself with the users' natural environments, and observing how they interacted with note-taking tools and techniques in real time. I then utilized software to effectively record and organize interview notes and ensure efficient data management. This then aided in the creation of debriefing notes and an affinity diagram using Miro, visualizing patterns and generating potential design ideas based on the findings.
